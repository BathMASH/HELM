3 The equation of a straight line through two points

The equation of the line passing through the points with coordinates A ( x 1 , y 1 ) and B ( x 2 , y 2 ) is given by the following formula.

Key Point 9

The line passing through points A ( x 1 , y 1 ) and B ( x 2 , y 2 ) is given by

y − y 1 y 2 − y 1 = x − x 1 x 2 − x 1 or, equivalently y − y 1 = y 2 − y 1 x 2 − x 1 ( x − x 1 )

Task!

Find the equation of the line passing through A ( − 7 , 11 ) and B ( 1 , 3 ) .

First apply the formula: y − y 1 y 2 − y 1 = x − x 1 x 2 − x 1

y − 11 3 − 11 = x + 7 1 + 7 .

Simplify this to obtain the required equation:

y = 4 − x
