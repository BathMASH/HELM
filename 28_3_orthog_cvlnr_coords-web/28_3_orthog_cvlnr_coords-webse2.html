2 Vector derivatives in orthogonal coordinates

Given an orthogonal coordinate system u , v , w with unit vectors û ̲ , v ̂ ̲ and ŵ ̲ and scale factors, h u , h v and h w , it is possible to find the derivatives ∇ ̲ f , ∇ ̲ ⋅ F ̲ and ∇ ̲ × F ̲ .

It is found that

grad f = ∇ ̲ f = 1 h u ∂ f ∂ u û ̲ + 1 h v ∂ f ∂ v v ̂ ̲ + 1 h w ∂ f ∂ w ŵ ̲

If F ̲ = F u û ̲ + F v v ̂ ̲ + F w ŵ ̲ then

div F ̲ = ∇ ̲ ⋅ F ̲ = 1 h u h v h w ∂ ∂ u ( F u h v h w ) + ∂ ∂ v ( F v h u h w ) + ∂ ∂ w ( F w h u h v )

Also if F ̲ = F u û ̲ + F v v ̂ ̲ + F w ŵ ̲ then

curl F ̲ = ∇ ̲ × F ̲ = 1 h u h v h w h u û ̲ h v v ̂ ̲ h w ŵ ̲ ∂ ∂ u ∂ ∂ v ∂ ∂ w h u F u h v F v h w F w

Key Point 6

In orthogonal curvilinear coordinates, the vector derivatives ∇ ̲ f , ∇ ̲ ⋅ F ̲ and ∇ ̲ × F ̲ include the scale factors h u , h v and h w .
