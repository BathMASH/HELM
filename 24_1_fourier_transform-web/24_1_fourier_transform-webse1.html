1 The Fourier transform

Unlike Fourier series, which are mainly useful for periodic functions, the Fourier transform permits alternative representations of mostly non-periodic functions.

We shall firstly derive the Fourier transform from the complex exponential form of the Fourier series and then study its various properties.
